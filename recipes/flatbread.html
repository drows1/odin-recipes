High Protein Flatbread

Description

This flatbread is very high in protein for the amount of calories, and it's easy and quick to make.

Ingredients for 2
  • 100g self-rising flour
  • 120g nonfat greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • pinch of salt
Steps
  1. Mix the flour, yogurt, garlic powder and salt into a bowl
  2. Knead the dough until harmonious and let it rest for 15 minutes
  3. Spray some olive oil in a cold nonstick pan, shape the dough in the pan into a flatbread
  4. Switch the heat to medium and flip it after 5 minutes, cook until the bread is golden brown
