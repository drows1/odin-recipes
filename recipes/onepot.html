One Pot Honey Soy Chicken & Rice

Description

A twist of the classic Chinese claypot rice made on the stovetop. Easy, quick and everything fits in one pot!

Ingredients for 4
  • 600g Chicken thigh, cut into 4cm pieces
  • 2 lap cheong sausages, sliced
  • 4 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp finely grated ginger
  • spring onions, sliced
  • 400g jasmine rice
  • 400ml chicken stock
  • 100ml Shaoxing wine
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp dark sweet soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp honey
Steps
  1. Soak the dried shiitake in hot water for 15 minutes. Remove and discard the stems, cut the shiitake in half
  2. Add the soy sauces, sesame oil and cornstarch to the chicken
  3. Mix and combine in a large heavy pot with a lid, the chicken, shiitake, sausage, garlic, ginger and rice
  4. Add the stock, wine and honey, stir until mixed thoroughly. Cover and place over high heat and bring to a simmer. Once simmering, turn the heat to low and cook gently for 30 minutes
  5. Remove the lid. Sprinkle the top with spring onion and a drizzle of sesame oil. Serve warm
