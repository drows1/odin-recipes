Spaghetti Aglio E Olio

Description

Enjoy a burst of Mediterranean flavors with this quick and easy dish containing only a few everyday ingredients.

Ingredients for 2 persons:

  • 150g Spaghetti
  • 50ml Extra virgin Olive Olio
  • 4 Cloves of garlic
  • chili pepper
  • Flat parley
  • Salt
  1. Put the pasta in boiling water, add salt to taste
  2. Cut the garlic and chili in thin slices, heat it up in a separate pan with olive oil
  3. Add some of the pasta water after the garlic and pepper turn fragrant
  4. Add the pasta to the pan 2 minutes before "al dente"
  5. Toss it around to emulsify, add more pasta water if necessary
  6. Remove it from the heat, add some chopped parley
